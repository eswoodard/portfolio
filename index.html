About me

My journey into tech has been an indirect one. My background is in mental health and I started my career providing services to individuals with behavioral health conditions. After several years I transitioned into the insurance industry and used my clinical knowledge to help manage the cost of medical care and ensure that indivduals received proper treatment. I have a Master's Degree in Counseling as well as an MBA.

Eventually I realized I needed to make a change. I remembered my fascination with computers as a teenager, and after spending some time teaching myself the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript, realized that this was something I could happily do all day, every day. I enrolled in a coding bootcamp and the rest is history.

Outside of web development, I love everything to do with Tolkien (the books, not the movies), anything Joss Whedon, good sci-fi/fantasy, and have a serious thing for Jane Austin. I love to hike and I'm a huge NFL fan. And I have two cats that I adore.
